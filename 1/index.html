James Doohan

More than just Scotty

Here's a time line of James Doohan's life:

  • 1920 - Born in Sarnia, Ontario‎, Canada
  • 1938 - Enrolled in the 102nd Royal Canadian Army Cadet Corps
  • 1940 - Begins training in England.
  • 1944 - Shoots two snipers on Juno Beach on DDay, later taking six shots from friendly fire.
  • 1946 - Changes focus in acting, attending a school with fellow stars such as Leslie Nielsen.

Here's a time line after he became Scotty:

  • 1966 - Star Trek - The Original Series
  • 1971 - Star Trek - The Animated Series
  • 1979 - Star Trek: The Motion Picture
  • 1982 - Star Trek II: The Wrath of Khan
  • 1984 - Star Trek III: The Search for Spock
  • 1986 - Star Trek IV: The Voyage Home
  • 1989 - Star Trek V: The Final Frontier
  • 1991 - Star Trek VI: The Undiscovered Country
  • 2005 - Died of pneumonia.

I got a letter from a young lady. It was a suicide note. I called her and said, “Hey, this is Jimmy Doohan, Scotty from Star Trek.” I said, “I’m gonna do a convention in Indianapolis. I wanna see you there.” Somebody had to help her, somehow. 8 Years later, I get a letter saying, “I do want to thank you so much for what you did for me.” That’s to me, the best thing I’ve ever done in my life.

-- James Doohan

James Doohan, or as he was better known for the latter part of his life, Scotty, was a war hero that became a hero and inspiration to many. To read more about him: Wikipedia entry.
